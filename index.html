Frequently Asked Questions

Still have questions? Message us on Viber anytime.

How do I book a unit?
Message us on Viber (+63 995 182 8466) or WhatsApp (+63 929 801 0815) to check availability. We confirm within minutes and send GCash/BDO Online payment details to lock in your dates.
Why should I book directly with you?
Booking direct means you get our best rates, instant confirmation, and exclusive monthly perks — including a lucky draw for a FREE extra night or a curated welcome gift. No middlemen, just personal service.
Do all units have a private bathroom?
Yes — every studio has its own en-suite private bathroom. You never share with other guests.
What's included in the rate?
WiFi, air conditioning (22 hours), linens, and towels. Swimming pool access is ₱150 per person per visit (payable at the building).
Can I check in early or check out late?
Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability. Message us when booking and we'll do our best.
Are the 7 Ortigas units in the same building?
Yes — all 7 are in Shine Residences, Meralco Ave, Pasig. Great if you're booking for a group or corporate team.
Can I pay in cash?
We prefer GCash or BDO Online for deposits. Cash on arrival may be arranged — please discuss when booking.
Is the swimming pool included?
The swimming pool at Shine Residences is available at ₱150 per person per visit — payable directly at the building. Most guests find they don't need it, but it's a great option if you'd like a dip!
